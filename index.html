About Delta Chapter

Delta Chapter is the Theta Tau chapter at Case Western Reserve University. We are the second oldest chapter in the nation, and we bring together students across engineering disciplines to develop professionally while forming strong personal bonds.

Founded in 1911, Delta focuses on supporting engineering majors through mentorship, career programming, and leadership opportunities. Our members participate in resume workshops, company site visits, and alumni panels to prepare for post-graduation careers.

As part of the national Theta Tau Professional Engineering Fraternity, we maintain a commitment to service, professional excellence, and brotherhood.
